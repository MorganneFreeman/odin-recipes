French Onion Soup

Description

French onion soup is a classic dish that is easy to make and enjoy on any occassion.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of yellow onions
  • sherry
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • french bread
  • swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Slice onions and saute in butter
  2. combine onions, beef broth and sherry
  3. place bread on top of soup
  4. place swiss cheese on top of bread
  5. broil until golden brown
  6. Enjoy!
