Tuna Salad Sandwich

Description

Tuna salad is a quick and easy lunch go-to for anyone on the go!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 dill pickle
  • 1 tbsp of mayo
  • 1 slice of bread

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Chop pickle into small pieces
  3. Open can of tuna
  4. Mix all ingredients in a small bowl
  5. Enjoy!
